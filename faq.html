RACE PACKS


When will I receive my Race Pack? For the Great North Run and our Marathon events the race packs will be sent out four weeks prior to the event. For all other Great Run events, packs are sent out approximately two weeks prior to the event.


What should be included in my Race Pack? Your race pack contain your RACE NUMBER and TIMING CHIP. If you have any questions about your race pack, please email info@greatrun.org.


I haven't received my Race Pack, what should I do? If your race pack does not arrive please email info@greatrun.org. The team will verify your entry and give you details as to how you can collect a replacement pack on race weekend. Competitor’s information guides can also be downloaded from the webpage of the event you have entered.

WITHDRAWAL POLICY


I can't take part in the event, can I get a refund? If your entry is accepted, your fee is not refundable unless supported by a doctor's note. Refunds will be accepted at least six weeks prior to the event (ten weeks if you have entered a Half Marathon or a Marathon event) otherwise we will be unable to stop your race pack being sent.


How do I withdraw my entry for the Great North Run? A person who has been accepted as an entrant into the 2018 Simplyhealth Great North Run who subsequently decides not to run prior to the event is eligible for a guaranteed place at the standard entry fee in the 2019 event. Once the entrant has committed to withdraw their entry you cannot be reinstated into the 2018 event. Your decision is final.


RESULTS


How do I view my Race Results? To view your race results please visit www.greatrun.org/results Make sure the correct event is highlighted and then you can search by your Race Number or Surname.


My time is not displayed in the Results section, how do I find my time? If no result is displayed please email info@greatrun.org with your full name, race number and the event in which you took part. We will then arrange for our results team to investigate. Please note timing chips must be worn in accordance with information printed within your pre-race information.
